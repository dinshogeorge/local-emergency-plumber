Hot water cylinder emergency services Auckland

The hot water systems have a major role in keeping up the warmth of our homes. When it starts to show signs of failing, it's an emergency. It is better to get it fixed urgently by the right professionals to save you from high water bills and other expenses without much delay. From high-end to low-end models, our crew of experienced plumbers deals with all kinds of hot water emergencies.

Symptoms of a faulty hot water cylinder

1. Faulty valve

Water leakage from the hot water cylinder or pipes is caused due to problems in the valve. Over time, Loose connections happen in the valve making large wastage of water thereby an increase in the water bill. Sometimes these valves can be tightened or replaced with a new one as early as you detect the problem. Seeking immediate professional assistance is the best way to deal with to avoid further damage to the hot water system.

2. High or low pressure

Fluctuation in water pressure can be a major concern for the failure of a hot water cylinder. Due to high pressure, the cylinder can even explode in the worst cases. With the accumulation of excessive sediments, the hot water cylinder could wear off soon. Once you notice any pressure difference, you must have an emergency checkup done by the plumbers.

3. High or low temperature

Inconsistency in the water temperature is another sign which indicates a faulty hot water cylinder. Sometimes it may be due to the ill-functioning of the thermostat or even the heating elements. Thermostats can be easily replaced whereas the problem in the heating element is a much bigger issue that needs quick and efficient plumbing assistance.

4. Rusty color of water

Hot water systems which are above 10 years may develop signs of corrosion more on the inside than the outside of the equipment in constant contact with water. On the hot water cylinder, there is a coating to slow down corrosion but with time the coating may fade and rust begins to form. If you notice discoloration or rust in the water it is important to have it replaced as urgently as possible.

5. Loud noises

If you notice any unusual sounds like rumbling and clanging noises from the water heater, it is an indication that your hot water cylinder is malfunctioning. The problem could be due to sedimentation or build-up of minerals in the drains, poor water flow with changing water pressure, or faulty valves. You can never know unless you hire professionals to check out your whole hot water system and help you out from this emergency situation.

6. High electricity bill

A high electricity bill is a clear sign that something is wrong with your water heater system. If the electricity bill is higher than usual you must keenly monitor the hot water cylinder which has high power consumption. Sometimes older water heating systems consume more energy even if there is nothing wrong with the system. So if your hot water cylinder is very old, it is better to upgrade it to the latest ones available which have high efficiency and help you cut down on your electricity bill.

Hot water cylinder replacement and installation

It is important to have your hot water cylinder checked and repaired if it shows any symptoms of failing. If the issues cannot be solved through repair, it is recommended to replace or install new hot water cylinders in place of the existing ones. Sometimes you need to replace only a single part but if it keeps failing, it is better to replace the whole system. As soon as the hot water cylinder develops warning signs, it is quite necessary to have an emergency call out to check and fix the problem. Our team of well-equipped plumbers deals with a wide range of hot water cylinder repairs, replacement, and installation according to the urgency of the issue.
